Telepresence

We will provide two OhmniLabs telepresence robots for attending ICAPS 2021 remotely.

The robots will be available for the following events:
  • Invited talks
  • All main conference sessions
  • Tutorials
  • Workshops
  • Community meeting

No cost will be associated with the use of the robots. Since we only have two robots, we ask people to please express a real interest in each slot they indicate during the registration process.

Please USE THIS FORM to register. Registration closes at the end of June 11, 2021.
