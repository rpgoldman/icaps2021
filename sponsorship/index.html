Levels of Sponsorship

Platinum ($10,000 ~ $20,000)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website before plenary talks and between streamed talks customization of sponsor’s page
  • Access to opt-in database of attendee profile information who are interested in being recruited
  • Virtual booth, one zoom license included
  • Chats with recruiters, researchers, and engineers
  • Links from all conference rooms to the sponsor’s booth
  • Thirty-two (32) complimentary full-access registrations for all official conference activities
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop, or demonstration
  • Choose Three of the named events/awards
Gold ($5,000 ~ $9,999)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website and before plenary talks
  • Access to opt-in database of attendee profile information who are interested in being recruited
  • Virtual booth, one zoom license included
  • Chats with recruiters, researchers and engineers
  • Links from rooms of workshops, tutorials, DC, Demonstrations, and Posters to the sponsor’s booth
  • Sixteen (16) complimentary full-access registrations for all official conference activities
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop, or demonstration
  • Choose two of the named events/awards
Silver ($2,500 ~ $4,999)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website and before plenary talks
  • Access to opt-in database of attendee profile information who are interested in being recruited
  • Eight (8) complimentary full-access registrations for all official conference activities
  • Virtual booth, one zoom license included
  • Links from rooms of tutorials, DC, Demonstrations, and Posters to the sponsor’s booth
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop, or demonstration
  • Choose one of the named events/awards

Named events/awards Sponsorship opportunities

  • Best Paper Award
  • Best Student Paper Award
  • Invited Speaker
  • Best Demo Award
