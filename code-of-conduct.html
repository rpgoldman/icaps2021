ICAPS 2021 Code of Conduct

It is against the ICAPS Code of Conduct to engage in discriminatory harassment against individuals participating at ICAPS. If you have been harassed or disparaged in violation of the ICAPS Code of Conduct, please inform the ICAPS 2021 Advocates Liangda Fang or Shirin Sohrabi either in person or through electronic means.
